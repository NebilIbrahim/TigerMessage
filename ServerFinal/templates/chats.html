About Tiger Message

Tiger Message is a princeton-based chat app that attempts to increase accessibility and useability. As it is available to all Princeton students and faculty, it isn't necessary to have accounts in various other messaging services to get started.

Extra Features

  • Subject Separation: Use the "restrict" button to limit chats to categories related to a provided subject.
  • Image Rendering: Post a link to an image ending with .jpg, .png, or .gif and it will render in the chat.
